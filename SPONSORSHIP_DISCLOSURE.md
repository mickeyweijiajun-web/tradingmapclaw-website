# Sponsorship & Affiliate Disclosure Policy

**English primary, Chinese translation follows each section (中文对照见每节之后).**
**Applies to:** any sponsored content, affiliate link, paid placement, or compensated collaboration involving TradingMapClaw, on any channel (website, X, LinkedIn, Reddit, Hacker News, YouTube, Product Hunt, GitHub, email/Substack).

---

## 1. Disclosure Must Be Prominent, Not Buried

Any sponsored content, paid placement, or affiliate relationship must be disclosed **at first mention**, in the same font size and visibility as the surrounding content — never only in a footer, never only in a linked terms page, never in light-gray small print.

Required disclosure language (use verbatim or closely adapted per channel length limits):

> **"This [post/video/email] includes a sponsored mention / affiliate link. TradingMapClaw received [compensation/free access/other consideration] from [Partner Name]. Our views remain independent — see Section 2."**

**中文对照：**
任何赞助内容、付费展示位或 affiliate 关系，必须在**首次出现处**披露，字体大小与周围内容一致——绝不能只写在页脚，不能只写在链接的条款页，不能用浅灰小字隐藏。

必须使用的披露语言（可按渠道字数限制适当调整，但含义不变）：

> "本[帖子/视频/邮件]包含赞助内容 / affiliate 链接。TradingMapClaw 已从 [合作方名称] 获得 [报酬/免费权限/其他对价]。我们的观点仍保持独立——见第二节。"

## 2. Independence Statement (Required Alongside Every Disclosure)

Every disclosure must be paired with an explicit independence statement:

> **"This disclosure does not change our research methodology or scenario labels. Sponsored content is clearly marked as such and never appears inside a Weekly Market Radar, Engine Disagreement Brief, or any dual-engine research output. Research and education only — not investment advice."**

**中文对照：**

> "本披露不会改变我们的研究方法或情景标签。赞助内容会明确标注，且绝不会出现在 Weekly Market Radar、Engine Disagreement Brief 或任何双引擎研究输出内部。仅供研究与教育用途——非投资建议。"

## 3. Non-Negotiable Boundaries (from FACTS.md §5 and PARTNERSHIP_POLICY.md)

- Sponsored content may **never** appear inside the WATCHLIST_ONLY research pipeline (Market Radar, Engine Disagreement Brief, System Build Log, or any of the 13 report types). Sponsorship is a separate, clearly bounded content category — never mixed into the research product itself.
- Sponsored/affiliate content must still carry the standard compliance footer: *"Research & education only. Not investment advice. · WATCHLIST_ONLY."*
- No sponsor's product or service may be described using buy/sell/hold language, "guaranteed," "beat the market," or any language prohibited elsewhere in FACTS.md §5 — sponsorship does not create an exception to the compliance red lines.
- No sponsor receives access to subscriber data, personal positions, account information, or any of the items listed as never-disclosed in FACTS.md §5.4.

**中文对照：**

- 赞助内容**绝不能**出现在 WATCHLIST_ONLY 研究管线内部（Market Radar、Engine Disagreement Brief、System Build Log 或任何 13 种报告类型之一）。赞助是独立且边界清晰的内容类别——绝不与研究产品本身混合。
- 赞助/affiliate 内容仍须带有标准合规声明："仅供研究与教育用途。非投资建议。· WATCHLIST_ONLY。"
- 任何赞助方的产品或服务，均不得使用买入/卖出/持有类指令语言、"稳赚"、"跑赢大盘"等 FACTS.md §5 中禁止的措辞——赞助不构成对合规红线的例外。
- 任何赞助方均不得获取订阅者数据、个人持仓、账户信息，或 FACTS.md §5.4 中列明的任何不可披露信息。

## 4. Approval Gate

Per `PARTNERSHIP_POLICY.md` §4, no sponsored or affiliate content is published without Mickey's explicit, per-post approval — the same DRAFT-by-default rule that governs all other public content applies here without exception.

**中文对照：**
依据 `PARTNERSHIP_POLICY.md` 第 4 节，任何赞助或 affiliate 内容在发布前，均需 Mickey 逐条明确批准——适用于所有其他公开内容的"默认 DRAFT"规则，在此没有例外。
