## 2. Description

TradingMapClaw is an independent AI-powered market intelligence and research operating system, built solo since May 2026 from zero coding background.

It runs a dual-engine architecture: Engine 1 (Hermes Agent) orchestrates fundamentals, valuation, insider, and macro/sentiment analysis. Engine 2 (Codex) independently checks technicals, capital flow, and options — and is required to cross-verify Engine 1's numbers before anything ships. A Multi-Model Council (DeepSeek V4 Pro, GLM-5.2, GPT-5.6) coordinates both engines, with a defined tiebreaker when they disagree.

Scale as of v2.0 (2026-07-12): 118 scheduled workflows (117 enabled), 230+ Python scripts, 50+ skills, 82 tracked assets across 5 groups, 13 report types, 12+ data sources — running on one Mac mini for roughly $7/month against a $55 cap.

This is research and education only — never investment advice. Every output is a scenario label: bullish, neutral, or bearish. Never a signal, never a pick, never a guarantee.

Free weekly intelligence (Market Radar, Engine Disagreement Brief, System Build Log) plus paid tutorials on how the system itself was built: https://www.tradingmapclaw.com
