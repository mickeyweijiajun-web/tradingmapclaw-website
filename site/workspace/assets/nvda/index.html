NVDA — NVIDIA Corporation

Semiconductors / AI

Research & education only. Not investment advice. · WATCHLIST_ONLY

STATUS: HERMES_READY

NVIDIA Corporation designs accelerated computing platforms centered on GPUs and the CUDA software ecosystem, with its data center hardware serving as the foundational compute layer for training and deploying large-scale AI models. The company holds a structurally dominant position across the AI infrastructure supply chain — from silicon design and HBM integration to high-speed networking (via Mellanox) and software stacks — making its quarterly revenue trajectory, gross margin trends, and inventory dynamics a widely followed proxy for hyperscaler AI capital expenditure demand. NVDA is maintained on the watchlist as a reference asset for monitoring AI supply chain health, fab capacity build-out cycles, and the downstream ripple effects of cloud and enterprise AI spending patterns.
