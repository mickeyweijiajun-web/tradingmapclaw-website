The System

Dual-Engine. Multi-Model Council.

Two AI engines — Hermes Agent and Codex — split the work across fundamentals, technicals, and macro, and cross-check each other before a council of models decides. Every part earns its place; nothing runs that can't be explained.

01 · Collect

12+ data sources

Prices, news, filings, social sentiment, market-structure signals, insider flow, and supply-chain mapping — pulled by 118 scheduled jobs.

02 · Reason

Two engines, split roles

Hermes Agent leads fundamentals, macro & sentiment; Codex runs technicals — and independently re-derives every key number before it ships.

03 · Verify

Maker / Checker

No single model is allowed to be right alone. A second engine independently re-derives the key numbers; divergence is surfaced, not hidden.

04 · Deliver

Two languages, two channels

English to Telegram, Chinese to Feishu — every report carries a compliance line. WATCHLIST_ONLY by architecture.

Engine 1 — Hermes Agent

The orchestrator and lead analyst. Runs the all-weather pipeline on one Mac mini (Apple Silicon), leads fundamentals, macro & sentiment, and drafts every report.

Engine 2 — Codex

The independent checker. Re-derives Engine 1's key numbers — valuation, growth, technicals — and prints any divergence instead of smoothing it over. The single most defensible feature in the system.
